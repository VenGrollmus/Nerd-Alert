Try writing an actual conversation here or just jot down more ideas.
User: Hello
Chatbot: Hello!
User: How are you?
Chatbot: I’m doing great, how are you?
User: I’m good
Chatbot: Good to know, is there anything I can help you with?
User: Can you tell me about sick leave?
Chatbot: yes, sure can.
Chatbot: Here is the link to the professional sick leave entitlement policy: [link]
Chatbot: Here is the link to the academic sick leave entitlement policy: [link]
Chatbot: Full-time staff earn 3.75 hours of sick each fortnight, total of 13 days per calendar year.
Chatbot: Sick leave is booked though the employee self-service portal online.
Chatbot: Sick leave taken 3 days or more consecutively will require a medical certificate to be sighted by the manager.
Chatbot: Did this answer your question?
User: Yes, thank you
Chatbot: No problem!
User: quit
Chatbot: Goodbye, Have a nice day
[End of conversation]

Trigger to start the chatbot conversation is a greeting from the user.
Trigger to end the chatbot conversation is quit or goodbye from the user.
