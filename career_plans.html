What common elements are there, if any?

The common elements if any are that each career requires a level of technological experience and understanding, especially the programming language component.

HRIS administrator and Software developer experience required is similar as HRIS requests a minimum of 2-3 years and software developer is 3+ years. It shows the positions have many diverse tasks and you need a certain time on the job to learn how to deal with those circumstances and tasks.

What differentiates each position from the others if anything?

A data analyst gathers and analysis data to determine certain risk to the business and to make certain recommendations based on the information. HRIS implement, support, and maintain systems to support the human resources department and serves as the technical point of contact.

How similar or different are your career plans across the group?

Data Analyst focuses on gathering data from software, Software developer is obviously focused on the backend such as data structures and software development.

HRIS Administrator requires data analysis skills to distribute data in addition to other skills to manage the system which stores the data.

There are similarities as a group as we all want to complete our degrees and move into the relevant areas we have indicated as our future career goals.
